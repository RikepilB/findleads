## Phase Goal

**As a** solo lead-generation user (Richard), **I want to** watch a scrape job through to
completion — with automatic resumption of a partial job, honest zero-result/failure states, and a
CSV of finished leads — **so that** I never have to babysit a stuck job or guess whether "no
results" means the scrape worked or silently broke, and can pull the leads out to start outreach.

<objective>
Build CSV export (`GET /api/jobs/:id/export`) for a completed job's leads, joined to `businesses`'
current CRM state (`notes`, `contacted`), with formula-injection sanitization applied to every
untrusted string column before the CSV is generated.

Purpose: this is the terminal step of the job lifecycle Phase 3/4 built — once a job is `done`,
Richard needs the leads out of Postgres and into a file he can actually work from, without that
export becoming a vector for a corrupted spreadsheet (CSV formula injection via place names,
addresses, or his own notes).
Output: `lib/csv/sanitize.ts`, `lib/csv/export.ts`, and `app/api/jobs/[id]/export/route.ts` — all
typechecking, unit- and integration-tested against a real Neon test database, plus the
`csv-stringify` dependency installed after an explicit legitimacy checkpoint.
</objective>
